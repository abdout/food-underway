## Platform Arrangement

**RestaurantOS** architecture is designed for optimal organization and scalability across multiple restaurant operations.

### System Components

#### Core Modules

##### Menu Management
- **Categories**: Appetizers, mains, beverages, desserts, specials
- **Item Details**: Photos, descriptions, prices, ingredients
- **Variations**: Sizes, toppings, customizations
- **Availability**: Time-based, stock-based, seasonal
- **Dietary Labels**: Vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher

##### Order Processing
- **Order Types**:
  - Dine-in with table management
  - Delivery with zone mapping
  - Pickup with time slots
  - Catering for events
- **Payment Methods**:
  - Cash on delivery/pickup
  - Credit/debit cards
  - Digital wallets
  - Restaurant credit
- **Order Flow**:
  - Customer places order
  - Restaurant confirms
  - Kitchen prepares
  - Delivery/service
  - Payment collection

##### Table Management
- **Floor Plans**: Visual layout editor
- **Table Types**: Regular, VIP, outdoor, bar
- **Capacity**: 2-seater to large groups
- **Status Tracking**:
  - Available
  - Occupied
  - Reserved
  - Needs cleaning
- **Reservation System**:
  - Online booking
  - Walk-in management
  - Waitlist queue

##### Customer Management
- **Profile Data**:
  - Contact information
  - Order history
  - Preferences
  - Allergies
- **Loyalty Programs**:
  - Points accumulation
  - Tier benefits
  - Rewards catalog
  - Birthday specials
- **Communications**:
  - SMS notifications
  - Email campaigns
  - WhatsApp updates
  - Push notifications

### Operational Workflow

#### Daily Operations

##### Opening Procedures
1. Staff clock-in via app
2. Kitchen inventory check
3. Daily specials setup
4. Table arrangement
5. System status verification

##### Service Hours
1. **Order Reception**:
   - Digital menu access
   - Order placement
   - Kitchen notification
   - Status updates

2. **Kitchen Operations**:
   - Order queue display
   - Preparation tracking
   - Quality control
   - Service notification

3. **Service Delivery**:
   - Table service
   - Delivery dispatch
   - Pickup alerts
   - Payment processing

##### Closing Procedures
1. End-of-day sales report
2. Inventory reconciliation
3. Staff clock-out
4. System backup
5. Next day preparation

### Staff Hierarchy & Roles

#### Management Level
- **Owner**: Full system access, financial reports, strategic decisions
- **Manager**: Operations oversight, staff management, reporting
- **Supervisor**: Shift management, quality control, escalations

#### Operational Staff
- **Chef**: Kitchen display, order management, inventory
- **Cashier**: Payment processing, order entry, customer service
- **Waiter**: Table management, order taking, service
- **Delivery**: Route management, order delivery, collection

#### Support Roles
- **Accountant**: Financial reports, reconciliation, billing
- **Marketing**: Campaign management, promotions, social media
