**Menu** is Saudi Arabia's premier cloud-based electronic menu platform. Designed specifically for Saudi restaurants and cafes to create stunning digital menus, manage orders efficiently, and grow their business—all with zero commission fees.

**منيو** هي المنصة الإلكترونية الرائدة للقوائم السحابية في المملكة العربية السعودية. مصممة خصيصاً للمطاعم والمقاهي السعودية لإنشاء قوائم رقمية مذهلة، وإدارة الطلبات بكفاءة، وتنمية أعمالهم - كل ذلك بدون عمولات.

### Highlights
</EN>

<AR>
### النقاط البارزة
</AR>

<EN>
- Multi-tenant by design: every record is scoped by `restaurantId`
- Custom subdomains per restaurant (e.g., `restaurant.menu.sa`)
- Arabic-first design with full RTL support and English localization
- Zero commission model: Keep 100% of your revenue
- WhatsApp Business API integration (70% of Saudi customers prefer WhatsApp)
- ZATCA-compliant e-invoicing and SFDA nutrition labeling
- Core features: QR menus, order management, table reservations, loyalty programs, customer analytics
- Saudi payment methods: Mada, STC Pay, Apple Pay, SADAD, cash on delivery
- Real-time order tracking and kitchen display system
- Advanced analytics with AI-powered insights
</EN>
